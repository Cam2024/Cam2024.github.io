During my freshman and sophomore years, I studied Electronic Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. In my junior and senior years, I pursued my studies in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Liverpool.

Throughout my academic journey, I had the privilege of working closely with my academic advisor, Professor Jeremy Smith, and my Final Year Project (FYP) supervisor, Dr. Angel Fernandez. Additionally, in 2022, I had the honor of participating in the SURF program, guided by Professor Ka Lok Man. In 2023, I also had the privilege of working in the Intelligent Unmanned Systems Laboratory under the guidance of Professor Shiyu Zhao.

In my spare time, my greatest hobby is developing legendary plans for non-existent future, aka daydreaming. Aside from the mundane stuff like biocomputers, brain-like computing, and interstellar travel, my most pressing project is to "design a brain smart enough" to earn me a PhD degree. While daydreaming is undoubtedly time-consuming, I manage to squeeze in some time for basketball and traveling.
